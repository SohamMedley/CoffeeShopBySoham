Our Story

Founded in 2025, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart in downtown Seattle. Our founder, Soham, had a vision to create a coffee experience that honored the farmers, celebrated the craft, and delighted customers.

Today, we source our beans directly from small farms across the globe, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices. Each batch is roasted in-house to bring out the unique character of every origin.
